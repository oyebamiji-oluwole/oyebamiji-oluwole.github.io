Dr. Oluwole Oyebamiji

Email: o.k.oyebamiji@bham.ac.uk

Research Interests

Oluwole’s expertise lies in theoretical foundations and multidisciplinary applications of Machine learning and Data mining, with a particular focus on Probabilistic Modelling, Numerical Optimisation, and cutting-edge computational techniques for analysing high-dimensional spatio-temporal data.

Education

  • Ph.D. Statistics, The Open University, Milton Keynes, UK, 2015.
  • MPhil. Statistics, University of Strathclyde, Glasgow, UK, 2010.

Experience

  • DeepMind Academic Fellow, School of Computer Science, University of Birmingham, 2023 – Present.
  • Senior Statistician, HR Wallingford Ltd, Wallingford, UK, 2021 - 2023.
  • Senior Research Associate, Department of Mathematics and Statistics, Lancaster University, 2018 – 2021.
  • Research Associate, School of Mathematics, Statistics and Physics, Newcastle University, 2015 - 2018.
