Research Interests

Oluwole’s expertise lies in theoretical foundations and multidisciplinary applications of Machine learning and Data mining, with a particular focus on Probabilistic Modelling, Numerical Optimisation, and cutting-edge computational techniques for analysing high-dimensional spatio-temporal data.
